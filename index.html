Security Services

Our specialised security services include the following

CORPORATE TACTICAL SERVICES (CTS) is a Gauteng Based company. CTS originated in 2017 and is a proudly owned South African company. Experience is drawn from diverse sectors.

Security Consulting

As a security firm, we are an independent organization with an outside view that helps various industries to meet their goals, mitigate challenges, solve problems and consult. All our consultants have expertise in a particular area and are contracted accordingly.

Corporate Security Solutions

Our security officers are employed by a private party to protect the employing party's assets from a variety of hazards by enforcing preventative measures. Security officers do this by maintaining a high-visibility presence to deter illegal and inappropriate actions, looking for signs of crime or other hazards, taking action to minimize damage, reporting any incidents, and informing emergency services, as appropriate.

Close Protection

CTS is a leading provider of executive protection services in South Africa. We protect individuals and groups from the risk of violence and kidnapping. We can provide a wide variety of personnel with varying levels of experience and expertise. Some of our staff members have extensive military and police experience. Close Protection Officers are trained to provide high-end security services. We have specially trained consultants who can provide our clients with the highest level of security and efficiency

Tactical Response Officers

Our Tactical Response Team (TRT) is a group of specially trained individuals that are utilized in a variety of requests from our clientele, to assist with the increase of violent crimes in South Africa, our Tactical Response teams are at the forefront of our organisation. Our professional team shows rapid deployment and ensures that our response makes a difference in all lives.

1. Management and supervision

Our company is committed to providing quality comprehensive service to our clients. Recommendations and reports will be compiled regularly and presented to the client. Monthly meetings between the client and a representative of our management team will be held to discuss security and safety-related matters

2. Re: Security Drivers

Road accidents and vehicle hijacking/robberies are some of the most dangerous factors that tourists and the general public face on a day-to-day basis in South Africa. Our security escorts and drivers are trained to handle these risks. The other risk factors that tourists and the general public face when traveling to and around South Africa are the lack of specialised transportation and the risk of getting lost in areas unknown to themselves. This issue can be easily solved by allowing a member of our staff to drive you or your group anywhere in South Africa
